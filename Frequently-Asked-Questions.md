**Q**: How do I remove the `Debug with x64dbg` entries from my context menu?

**A**: Download & execute [this](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/x64dbg/x64dbg/master/x64dbg_shell_remove.reg) regfile (remember to `Save as...`).
